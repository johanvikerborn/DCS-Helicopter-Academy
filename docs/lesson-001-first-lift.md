# Lesson 001 - First Lift

Difficulty: ★☆☆☆☆

Aircraft: UH-1H Huey

Estimated Time: 15 Minutes

---

## Objectives

At the end of this lesson you should be able to:

- Lift into a stable hover
- Maintain heading
- Control altitude
- Translate in all directions
- Perform a safe landing

---

## Theory

The helicopter is controlled using three primary controls.

### Collective

Controls rotor blade pitch.

Increasing collective increases lift.

Increasing collective also requires pedal corrections.

---

### Cyclic

Controls the direction of the rotor disk.

The helicopter moves in the direction the rotor disk is tilted.

---

### Pedals

Control tail rotor thrust.

Pedals control heading.

Pedals do NOT turn the helicopter like a car.

They counter torque and control yaw.

---

## Exercise 5

---

## Common Mistakes

### Overcontrolling

Small corrections.

Do not chase every movement.

---

### Looking Too Close

Look at the horizon.

Not directly below the aircraft.

---

### Fighting The Aircraft

The helicopter naturally drifts.

Anticipate movement.

Do not react late.

---

## Success Criteria

- Hover maintained for 20 seconds
- Heading within ±15 degrees
- Safe landing
